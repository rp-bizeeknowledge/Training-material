Core Rule

Every customer interaction generates a ticket number, regardless of whether it involves an active customer or a sales inquiry.

Agents are expected to guide customers through Bizee's process at all times and ensure our services are clearly and consistently positioned. Under no circumstances should agents direct customers to contact the Secretary of State for filings that can be completed through Bizee.

Exceptions apply only when Bizee does not offer the service for the customer's specific state, such as certain reinstatements or dissolutions, or when state contact information is already provided within the dashboard or an Order Issue.
