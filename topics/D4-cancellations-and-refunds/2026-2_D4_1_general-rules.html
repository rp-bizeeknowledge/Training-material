General Rules

The Do's and Don'ts for Cancellations and Refunds
Before You Start

Take a moment to carefully review the order history, order status, DB comments and Zendesk internal notes. This helps prevent mistakes and ensures you have the full context before taking any action.

If you see Holds or ongoing Order Issues:

Do not resolve Identity Confirmation Issues. Escalate to your supervisor and request cancellation.

If the hold stems from a Name or Additional Information Issue, you can resolve the issue and then cancel and refund the order; no escalation is required in this case.

Taking Action
Perform Cancellation & Refund actions as follows:

  • Subscriptions are cancelled from the Virtual Address, Agent or Subscriptions tab in DB/Bizee Tech (depending on the service), and refunded through the Transaction History section, since the refund needs to be applied over a charge processed on a specific date.

Subscription cancellation · Example

  • Main and Miscellaneous Orders are cancelled and refunded from the Order Status dropdown.

Main order cancellation & refund · Example
Setting Accurate Expectations

Never confirm that something is cancelled or refunded until it's actually done. If you need time to verify, place the customer on hold, offer a callback, or let them know you'll follow up by email.

Always inform the customer that funds typically take 3 to 5 business days to process, once a refund is applied.

Accurate Documentation

Ensure that a concise and comprehensive DB comment and Zendesk internal note are left after completing any required actions and confirming that all changes have been successfully applied.

C.A.N.C.E.L.

Use C.A.N.C.E.L. when processing a refund or cancellation request.

Always Follow This Order
C

Check the order history and comments

Review DB comments, order history, and Zendesk notes before taking any action.

A

Assess the order status

Determine the current status and whether cancellation is permitted based on it.

N

Navigate to the correct tab

Use the correct tab in DB/Bizee Tech based on the order type (subscription, main or miscellaneous).

C

Cancel using the appropriate option

Select Cancel/Refund from the status dropdown, or use the red Cancel button for subscriptions.

E

Explain the refund timeline

Inform the customer that funds take 3 to 5 business days to process once the refund is applied.

L

Leave a clear comment and internal note

Document all actions taken in DB comments and the Zendesk internal note.
