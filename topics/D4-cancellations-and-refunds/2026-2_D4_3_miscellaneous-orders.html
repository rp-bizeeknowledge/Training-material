Rules
Rules
1
Order Issues
If the order has an active Order Issue, click on "resolve issue" before you cancel the order, or the system will not allow you to proceed.
2
Communicate Refund Timeframe
The estimated timeframe for refunds is between 3 to 5 business days.
3
Confirm Cancellation & Refund
Do not provide Cancellation or Refund confirmation until the process is complete.
Steps
1

Go through the Internal Storage, Comment Section and Order History Tab. You cannot move forward if there's any indication that the order has been submitted to the state or a comment stating the order should not be cancelled or refunded.

2

Check the Order Status and Order History Tab. If the status is:

Review

The Order can be cancelled and refunded.

Hold
  • If currently on hold, but at any point it has been forwarded to the state: Cancel and refund only if there are no comments stating the order should not be cancelled or refunded.
    Miscellaneous Orders in Hold Status · Example
  • If there is no comment (usually pinned) advising to not cancel the order, then proceed with refunding.
    Miscellaneous Orders with 'DO NOT CANCEL AND REFUND' Comment · Example
  • If never forwarded to the state: Cancel and refund.
Processing

Do not cancel or refund.

Forwarded

Do not cancel or refund.

Shipped

Do not cancel or refund.

Transferred

Do not cancel or refund.

3

Place it on Hold and refresh DB/Bizee Tech to ensure there are no last-minute updates from Filing.

4

Expand the status dropdown in the panel on the left, then click the Cancel/Refund option.

Cancelling and Refunding Miscellaneous Order · Example
5

Leave a clear comment about the actions taken, along with an internal note on your ticket.

Special Scenarios
Annual Report + State Filings Bundle Orders

When you cancel an Annual Report order that includes a State Filings Bundle subscription, a confirmation pop-up will appear.

You will have to confirm with the customer whether they would like to keep the subscription or not, and select the corresponding option:

Cancelling and Refunding AR + SFB Order · Example

Confirm with the customer — which option applies?

Cancel / Refund

If they want to cancel the order in full — this will cancel the SFB subscription and refund the whole amount paid.

C/R & Keep Subscription

If they want to cancel the Annual Report and keep the subscription active — this will only refund the state fee, excluding the $99 subscription portion.

Review the flowchart in the Knowledge Base to determine which paths apply and offer only the available options before promising any outcomes.
