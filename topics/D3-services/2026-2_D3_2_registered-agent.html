Registered Agent

A service to handle a company's State correspondence
What It Is

A Registered Agent is a person or company designated to receive official state correspondence and legal documents on behalf of a business.

Every state requires a business to appoint and continuously maintain a Registered Agent that meets the following criteria:

Be based on a physical address within the state where the business is formed.

Someone must be available at that address Monday–Friday, 9 a.m.–6 p.m.

No P.O. Boxes or mail centers allowed.

This is required from formation, and throughout the entire life of the business. Without one, the company may fall out of good standing and become subject to administrative dissolution.

Service Overview

When our company acts as the Registered Agent for a business, the name "REPUBLIC REGISTERED AGENT LLC" or "REPUBLIC REGISTERED AGENT INC" (depending on the state) appears on the company's formation documents and in the state's public records.

The Registered Agent service is available to all customers, including those who did not form their business with Bizee.

When a company is formed through us, the service is included at no cost for the first year and then renews automatically at $149 after that period.

If purchased as a standalone service, the cost is $149 at checkout. The service covers the receipt of official state correspondence and legal notices only.

Each company must have its own Registered Agent; the subscription cannot be shared across multiple businesses.

Subscription Details

This subscription renews automatically unless canceled by the customer before the renewal date.

We do not send subscription reminders.

Cancellations are not immediate but take effect at the end of the current billing period.

If no refund is requested after renewal, the service will continue until the next renewal date.

Mail Handling Process
Signature Request

Customers sometimes need a Signature from the Registered Agent on a specific form to complete a state filing. Customers should complete and sign the form first, then email it to support@incfile.com. We'll take care of the Registered Agent signature and help them move forward.

1

Customer's request: Customer completes and signs the form, then submits it by email to support@incfile.com, explaining they need the Registered Agent's signature.

2

Escalation: The request is escalated to the appropriate team for review and processing.

3

Requested signature provided: The Registered Agent signature is added and the completed form is returned to the customer to move forward.

Conversion

If a customer converts their business (for example, from an LLC to a C Corp), we can still serve as their Registered Agent as long as they provide the conversion documents for review.

If the client already has an existing company (whether formed through us or independently) and wants us to become their Registered Agent, we will file a Change of Agent (CoA) with the state on their behalf.

$149 + state fee

Example: A client with an LLC in Texas wants to switch from their current agent to us. They would pay $149 plus the applicable Texas state fee for the COA filing.

2 $149 + Customer Files CoA

If the customer wants to handle the CoA filing themselves, they can purchase our Registered Agent service for $149 at checkout. They are then responsible for submitting the Change of Agent to the state and paying any required state fees separately.

$149 only

This applies when:

  • They are forming a business on their own, but want to appoint us as their agent
  • They have an existing company and prefer to file the COA personally

Example: A customer forming a corporation in California on their own can list us as their Registered Agent, pay $149 at checkout, and file the paperwork directly with the state.

3 Free (1st Year)

When we are forming the company, our Registered Agent service is free for the first year.

$0 — Year 1

Example: A customer forming an LLC in Florida through our platform would only pay the cost to form a company in Florida (pricing varies per package). The Registered Agent service is included at no extra cost for the first year.

Note: "State Fee" refers to what the state charges. This could be a fee for changing the agent or, in the case of a new formation, the standard formation filing fee. It varies by state and by filing type.
