Bizee Tax Subscription & Free Consultation

Vyde partnership · 30-min consultation · Subscription packages · Cancellation notes
What It Is

Bizee does not directly handle tax filings for its customers. However, it offers a complimentary 30-minute tax consultation through Vyde, an independent accounting partner. This one-time session is included with a Bizee order and is designed to help customers understand their tax obligations, explore potential deductions, and receive expert guidance on managing business taxes.

Separately, Vyde also provides subscription-based services under the label "Bizee Tax." This is not presented as a Vyde product, but rather as a branded Bizee offering. The subscription includes a 30-day free trial, with two package options available, and offers continued access to tools and professional support for business tax management.

5.1 Service Overview
ℹ️

While both services are powered by Vyde, it's important to note that they are entirely separate: the free 30-minute consultation is a one-time service provided with the customer's Bizee order, while the Bizee Tax subscription is an optional, recurring service customers can choose to enroll in through their dashboard.

The consultation can be scheduled by potential customers through our website, while Bizee customers can schedule it directly via their dashboard.
Vyde can only call U.S. phone numbers and does not make long-distance calls.
"Bizee Tax" will appear as the provider, but Vyde handles the service.

If a customer inquires about any of the features listed above, refer them to Vyde. When customers are already enrolled in this service, they are assigned to a dedicated Tax Consultant, who will be responsible for addressing any questions. Their contact information is available in the Taxes tab of the dashboard.

🤝

Be sure not to describe Vyde as a separate department but as our partner instead.

5.2 Notes

This is the only subscription customers can cancel directly through their dashboard.

If a customer wants to cancel a subscription they didn't buy from us, ask how much they were charged. If it's not $125 or $249 (for example, $99 or another amount), tell them to contact Vyde directly. Vyde also offers personal tax services that customers may have signed up for after their initial contact through the dashboard. Customers can find Vyde's phone number in their dashboard or in emails from Vyde after signing up.
