State Filings Bundle Subscription

Unlimited state filings in a yearly subscription
What It Is

For a one-time payment of $99, customers can enroll in a yearly subscription that allows unlimited state filings. They only pay the state fees, waiving any processing fees from us. This is a cost-effective option for customers who expect to file multiple documents throughout the year.

The purpose of the subscription is to reduce or eliminate processing fees, making state compliance more affordable.

Service Overview

Once enrolled in the subscription, customers can submit the following filings without paying our processing fees:

Annual Reports
Amendments
Certificate of Good Standing (COGS)
Change of Registered Agent
Dissolution Filings
DBA Filings
Foreign Qualification Services (FQS)
Reinstatements
Subscription Details
  • The $99 subscription is per company, not per user.
  • The subscription includes Foreign Qualification filings. Example: A company registered in Texas with a Foreign Qualification in California can file annual reports or amendments for the California entity without additional processing fees.
Important Notes

Filings are not automatically submitted; customers must initiate the specific filings they need.
