Domain & Email Subscription

Open SRS · Pricing tiers · Renewals & cancellations · Escalations
What It Is

Customers who form their company through us receive access to our Domain and Email Services provided in partnership with Open SRS, which allow them to register the company's domain and create a business email account. The basic subscription tier is free for the first year and renews automatically after that. This feature is not available as a stand-alone through the website.

4.1 Service Overview

The subscription is activated once the domain name has been registered. Pricing options are:

2 Mailboxes: $36 per year (First year provided for free with formation package)
3 additional mailboxes: $60
10 additional mailboxes: $120
25 additional mailboxes: $180
Privacy Protection: $12 per year (optional)
4.2 Renewals and Cancellations
🔄

Services automatically renew each year. Automatic renewal cannot be disabled.

💰

Refunds are available only within 3 business days of activation or renewal.

⚠️

Cancelling a domain also cancels its attached email service and all access is lost immediately.

🆓

Free domains cannot be refunded, but if cancelled within 3 business days of activation, a new domain can be created at no additional cost.

4.3 Customer Service, Support and Escalations

The Domain & Email Team provides all required technical support for this subscription via email, with response times ranging from 1 to 2 business days. Phone support is not available.

If a customer requests assistance, escalate the case and include the following:

Order Number
Company Name
Domain Name
Brief description of the Issue
Screenshots of the Issue (if possible)
Customer's email address on file
4.4 Important Notes
Domain and email support is email-only.
Refunds are available within 3 business days from activation or renewal.
Customers cannot cancel domain and email services directly from their dashboard.
