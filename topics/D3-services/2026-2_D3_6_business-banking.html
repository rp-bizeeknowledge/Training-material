Business Banking

Bank of America · Relay · Eligibility · BoA exclusive packages
What It Is

Bizee partners with Bank of America and Relay to help customers open business bank accounts. Bank of America is for U.S. residents. Relay is for foreign customers and those preferring an online banking solution.

Bizee does not open bank accounts on behalf of a business.

6.1 Relay Offer

Relay is an online banking option for:

Foreign customers
Customers who opt out of the Bank of America offer
Key Benefits
Fully online sign-up (takes under 10 minutes)
Available in the Financial Tab of the Bizee dashboard
Important Notes

Bizee does not assist with account setup or application.

Approval is not guaranteed by Relay.

Customers must meet Relay's eligibility requirements to open an account.

6.2 Bank of America Offer

Customers need a Bizee account to access this offer. A Bank of America rep will contact them directly to complete the process.

Key Points
Bank of America offers business banking solutions to U.S. residents.
Customers will be guided by a Bank of America representative through the setup process.
Bizee does not open or manage bank accounts on behalf of customers.
The process can be completed over the phone or in person, depending on the business type.
Important Notes

Foreign customers cannot open a Bank of America business account.

Processing time varies, depending on the business and documentation required.

Customers should have their EIN, legal business name, and formation documents ready.

BoA Packages

We are Bank of America's exclusive partner for business formation services. When a visitor on Bank of America's website explores small business banking options, they are presented with two packages we offer through Bizee:

Basic Plus

Simplifies business formation, including EIN filing, for Bank of America customers. Includes an exclusive discount (typically $70 off) on business formation through our platform.

Standard Plus

Enhanced formation package for Bank of America customers. Includes an exclusive discount (typically $150 off) on business formation through our platform.

To explore packages, pricing and features, visit: bankofamerica.bizee.com/form-order-now.php
