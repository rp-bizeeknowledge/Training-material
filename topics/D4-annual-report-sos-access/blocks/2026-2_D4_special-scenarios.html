4.1 — Miscellaneous Products (In-House)
Conditional

4.1.1 EIN as Add-On

Rules: Refunds are only applicable to Basic Orders with added EIN procurement. Cancellation and refund is not available if we have obtained the EIN, if the fax application for the SS-4 has been sent, or if the EIN procurement is part of a Standard or Premium Order.

Steps:

  1. Go to the Transaction History Tab in DB/Bizee Tech.
  2. Issue a partial refund of $70 from the total amount for the order. Make sure to select "Other" from the Reason Dropdown options.

If the scenario you encounter is not mentioned above, escalate by checking off the "EIN/SS4 Escalation" box via Zendesk.

Review Status Only

4.1.2 EIN-Only (Stand-Alone)

Rules:

  • EIN-only orders can be cancelled and refunded exclusively while in Review Status, regardless of whether it pertains to a domestic or foreign applicant.
  • A refund is only available if no fax has been sent (if applying through an SS-4), and no EIN was generated through automation.
  • Do not cancel or refund if the order has been Shipped. (These orders will only go from Review to Shipped.)
  • If six months have passed since the customer initially signed the SS-4 form, the EIN has not been obtained, and the customer requests a refund or raises concerns about the delay: issue a refund for the EIN obtainment service fee, and escalate the case to the EIN department only if the customer confirms they would like us to submit additional procurement attempts.

If the scenario isn't mentioned above, escalate by checking off the "EIN/SS4 Escalation" box in Zendesk. Inform the customer you'll respond within 1–2 business days.

Steps:

  1. Place it on Hold and refresh DB/Bizee Tech to ensure there are no recent updates.
  2. Expand the Status dropdown on the left, then click the Cancel/Refund option.
  3. Leave a clear comment in DB/Bizee Tech, and an internal note on Zendesk.
Review Status Only

4.1.3 Form 2553

Rules:

  • Form 2553 orders can be cancelled and refunded exclusively while in Review Status.
  • Refunds are not available if the 2553 is part of a Main Order for an S-CORP, Standard, and Premium packages for LLCs and Corps.
  • We can only cancel and refund if there is no fax receipt. Always go through the Comment Section and the Document Upload Tab to confirm.

Steps:

  1. Place it on Hold and refresh DB/Bizee Tech to ensure there are no recent updates.
  2. Expand the Status dropdown on the left, then click the Cancel/Refund option.
  3. Leave a clear comment in DB/Bizee Tech, and an internal note on Zendesk.

Special Case — Formation Packages: If Form 2553 is part of a formation package (e.g., Standard or Premium) for an LLC or Corporation and the customer no longer wants it: (1) Check the Document Escalation box. (2) Submit as Pending, requesting removal of the form from the dashboard.

Conditional

4.1.4 Business Contract Templates

Rules: No refunds if the templates were included in a package. If purchased separately, visit the order's dashboard and click on the contract template section. Try to preview the PDF/DOCX. If the templates have not been downloaded, a warning message will appear. If the warning appears, you can proceed with cancellation and refund. If no warning appears and you can see the templates, then a cancellation and refund are not possible.

Steps:

  1. Go to the order's dashboard.
  2. If the warning appears, remove the Contract Templates from the list in DB/Bizee Tech and provide the refund.
  3. Leave a clear comment in DB/Bizee Tech, and an internal note on Zendesk.
Conditional

4.1.5 BOIR (Beneficial Ownership Information Report)

Rules: Refunds are available if the BOIR was not completed (Shipped) while the service was offered. Do NOT offer BOIR refunds proactively.

Steps:

  1. Expand the Status dropdown on the left panel in DB/Bizee Tech, then click the Cancel/Refund option.
  2. Leave a clear comment in DB/Bizee Tech, and an internal note on Zendesk.
4.2 — Miscellaneous Services (In-House)
Up to 3 months

4.2.1 Virtual Address within a Main Order

Rules — Always verify VA usage first:

  • Go through the company's Formation Documents and records on the State's Website to confirm whether the VA is listed as the company's, members', or directors' address.
  • If listed: Inform the customer that they need to file an Amendment to update the information with the state after cancellation.
  • If not listed: Confirm the new address in writing and update DB/Bizee Tech. An Amendment is not needed.

Agents may issue up to 3 months ($87 total) as a courtesy refund. If the customer asks for more, do not issue a refund — tell them you will send the request for further review. In Zendesk, escalate by selecting your supervisor's name from the dropdown. Do not promise any refund amount until it is approved.

Always ask for the cancellation reason, and set the Ticket Type to Virtual Address, the Virtual Address Type to Cancellation, and select the most accurate reason from the Cancellation Reason dropdown before solving the ticket.

Steps:

  1. Go to the Virtual Address Tab in DB/Bizee Tech.
  2. Click the red Cancel button next to the active VA subscription. If there are multiple active subscriptions, make sure the correct state is selected.
  3. If a refund is applicable, go to the Transaction History Tab and locate the $29 VA charge. You may only issue refunds for the 3 most recent VA transactions.
Up to 3 months

4.2.3 Virtual Address as a Stand-Alone

VA Child Orders will automatically create a Ghost Order (Parent Order). The Child Order will have "Transferred" as the status. Agents will need to apply these cancellations from the Ghost (Parent) Order.

Rules — Verify VA usage (same as above). Additionally verify if the order included filing an Amendment:

  • If the amendment was included and filed: Cancel and refund the VA fee only (up to 3 months).
  • If the amendment was included and NOT filed: Cancel and refund in full.
  • If the amendment was NOT included: Cancel and refund in full.

Steps:

  1. Go to the Subscriptions Tab for the Parent Order in DB/Bizee Tech.
  2. Click the red Cancel button next to the active VA subscription. If there are multiple active subscriptions, make sure the correct state is selected.
  3. If a refund is applicable, go to the Transaction History Tab, locate the applicable $29 VA charge and click Refund. You may only issue refunds for the 3 most recent VA transactions.
Within 3 Months of Purchase

4.2.4 Registered Agent within a Main Order

Note: When a Registered Agent service is cancelled, we stage the resignation but do not resign before the active term ends. Service continues until the expiration date, and resignation is triggered only after that date. This differs from other services (domains, email, virtual address), which cancel immediately.

Rules — Always verify RA usage:

  • Check the company's records on the State's Website to confirm if we are listed as the company's RA.
  • If listed: It is mandatory to inform the customer that they need to file a Change of Agent to update the information with the state after, to avoid losing good standing for failure to maintain an Agent on File.
  • If not listed: Update DB/Bizee Tech with the new information.

Agents should only select "Client Cancelled the RA Service" in the cancellation reason field in DB/Bizee Tech. You may only issue a refund for one year of service ($119) if requested within 3 months of the purchase/renewal.

Steps — follow the correct procedure depending on the specific case:

  • Cancel and Refund (RA was NOT updated with SOS): Cancel the service directly on the Transaction History tab by selecting refund on the latest RA charge.
  • Cancel and Refund (RA WAS updated with SOS): Cancel the service on the Transaction History tab, select refund on the latest RA charge. Set the registered agent to "no agent" and update DB/Bizee Tech to match the SOS website.
  • Cancel No Refund (RA not updated with SOS): Set the registered agent in Stage for Resignation.
  • Cancel No Refund (RA updated with SOS): Set the registered agent to "no agent" and update DB/Bizee Tech to match the SOS website.

After cancelling, if the company is active on the SOS website and we are still listed as the RA, it is mandatory that you inform the customer that we will be resigning as their Registered Agent, and unless they update the information with the state, their company can get administratively dissolved.

⚠️

Multiple Years Refund: If a customer reinstated multiple years' worth of the RA subscription in a single payment (e.g., $238, $357, $476), escalate with your supervisor. DB/Bizee Tech won't allow partial refunds for RA renewal charges — refunds are issued by check. Escalate the check refund request using the supervisor escalation dropdown.

⚠️

Reverting a Cancellation: If the customer retracts after cancelling and the RA was marked "Staged for Resignation," no refund was given, and the service has not expired — change the RA from "Staged for Resignation" to "Yes" on the Agent tab of DB/Bizee Tech. In all other cases, the customer can reinstate from their dashboard.

4.2.5 Registered Agent as a Stand-Alone

RA Child Orders will automatically create a Ghost Order (Parent Order). Cancellations must be done from the Ghost (Parent) Order.

Rules — Verify RA usage (same as above). Additionally verify if the order included filing a Change of Agent:

  • If the CoA was included and filed: Cancel and refund the RA fee only.
  • If the CoA was included and NOT filed: Cancel and refund the order in full.
  • If the CoA was NOT included: Cancel and refund the order in full.

Steps:

  1. Go to the Agent Tab for the Parent Order in DB/Bizee Tech.
  2. Click the blue Edit button and set the Registered Agent Service field to "Staged for Resignation".
  3. If a refund is applicable, go to the Transaction History Tab, locate the applicable RA charge, and click Refund.
Within 30 Days

4.2.6 State Filings Bundle (SFB)

Rules:

  • You are authorized to issue a refund only if the customer has NOT rendered any Miscellaneous Orders after subscribing to the bundle.
  • Refunds are only available within 30 days from the original charge.
  • If a customer requests a refund but has a Miscellaneous Order that used the bundle: a refund is allowed only if the Miscellaneous Order was previously cancelled. If the customer wants to cancel the Miscellaneous Order, follow the corresponding cancellation procedure first, then process the SFB refund.
  • Cancelling with no refund is available; this deactivates the automatic renewal, and the service remains active until one year from the customer's sign-up date.

Steps:

  1. Go to the Subscriptions Tab in DB/Bizee Tech.
  2. Click the red Cancel button next to the active State Filings subscription.
  3. If a refund is applicable, go to the Transaction History Tab, locate the applicable $99 charge, and click Refund.
4.3 — Co-Sourced Products
Requires Unikel Law Auth

4.3.1 Trademarks

Rules:

  • Trademarks ordered after August 1, 2022, are handled by Unikel Law.
  • Trademarks never go to Forwarded Status. Once in Processing, the team is most likely about to contact the customer for their request.
  • If the customer claims they have not been contacted in more than two weeks, assess whether they want to move forward and escalate accordingly.
  • If only a partial refund is authorized, they will specify the amount in the side conversation.
  • Always inform the customer that you have escalated the request and keep them updated.

Steps:

  1. Use the macro "Trademark Escalation - Side Conversation - Unikel Law" to create a side conversation with the provider and fill out the requested information.
  2. Once authorization is received, expand the Status dropdown on the left panel in DB/Bizee Tech, then click the Cancel option.
  3. Go to the Transaction History, locate the corresponding charge, and process the refund for the authorized amount.
Requires Wolters Kluwer Auth

4.3.2 BLRP

Rules: BLRP cancellation & refund requests have to be authorized by Wolters Kluwer. Refunds are available only if the research has not been conducted or delivered within the promised timeframe.

Standalone Orders — Steps:

  1. Use the macro "Wolters Kluwer Email - BLRP" to create a side conversation with the provider.
  2. Once authorization is received, expand the Status dropdown and click Cancel.
  3. Go to the Transaction History and locate the corresponding $99 charge.

Included in a Main Order — Steps:

  1. If two weeks have passed since the Date of Formation and the BLRP has not been delivered, use the macro "Wolters Kluwer Email - BLRP" to create a side conversation.
  2. Once authorization is received, go to the Transaction History and issue a $99 refund from the total order amount.
Requires FastKits Auth

4.3.3 Business Formation Kit

Rules: Cancellation & refund requests have to be authorized by FastKits. Refunds are available if the order is under review, processing, or hold status.

Steps:

  1. Use the macro "Kit Cancellation Email - Fastkit Email" to create a side conversation with the provider.
  2. Once authorization is received, expand the Status dropdown and click the Cancel option.
  3. Go to the Transaction History Tab, locate the applicable $99 charge, and click Refund.
4.4 — Co-Sourced Services
Within 3 Business Days of Renewal

4.4.1 Domain & Email

Rules:

  • Refunds only apply when the Domain and Email feature was renewed less than 3 business days ago.
  • If renewed more than 3 business days ago, you can cancel the service but do not refund the renewal fee.
  • You can cancel the Domain & Email subscription at any time. Access to all domain and email features stops right after cancellation is processed. Always let customers know this before cancelling so they have a chance to run a backup if needed.

If unsure how to proceed, escalate your ticket to the Domain and Email Department through the checkbox in Zendesk.

Steps:

  1. Send the "Domain and Email – Cancellation Request" macro or use it as a guide to explain domain registration limitations and immediate email service disruption. Obtain written or verbal confirmation before proceeding.
  2. Go to the Subscriptions Tab in DB/Bizee Tech.
  3. Click the red Cancel button next to the active Domain & Business Email subscription. If there are multiple active subscriptions, make sure the correct domain is selected.
  4. If a refund is applicable, go to the Transaction History Tab and issue the refund from the corresponding D&E charge.
Within 30 Days of Charge

4.4.2 Bizee Tax

Rules:

  • This subscription can be cancelled by the customer through their Dashboard (Taxes tab → scroll to subscription section).
  • Refunds can only be issued for the most recent transaction, up to one month total.
  • Customers have 30 natural days from the date of the charge to request a refund.
  • If the cancellation request comes through email or the customer prefers you handle the cancellation over the phone, do not cancel it from the dashboard. Agent cancellations must follow the steps below.

Steps:

  1. Go to the Subscriptions Tab in DB/Bizee Tech.
  2. Click the red Cancel button next to the active Bizee Tax subscription.
  3. If a refund is applicable, go to the Transaction History Tab and issue the refund from the corresponding Bizee Tax charge.
