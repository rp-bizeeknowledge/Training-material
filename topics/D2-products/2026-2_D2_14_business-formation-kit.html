Business Formation Kit

The Business Formation Kit is a professional customized binder
What It Is
1
Binder & Slip Case

Our standard LLC incorporation or corporate kits are handcrafted in the traditional bookbinding method and constructed with heavier materials for extra durability and long life. The three-ring mechanisms with double opening and closing boosters allow pages to lie flat and turn easily.

2
20 Member Stock Certificates

These elegantly designed numbered security certificates contain your company name and state of incorporation and are printed on the finest security paper available.

3
Corporate Forms / Documents

Your personalized binder will include printed minutes and bylaws or an operating agreement if they are included in the package you chose.

4
Corporate Embossing Seal

Custom 1 5/8 metal die cast pocket seal embosser personalized with your company name, state of formation and date of formation.

5
Personalized Business Name on Kit

Each quality binder is personalized in gold with your organization name and comes with a matching slipcase.

6
Transfer Ledger

8-page alphabetized transfer ledger included with your kit.

7
Index Tabs

Mylar-coated, easy to use tabs for your Corporate or LLC Kit.

A document set designed to organize and store essential business records, including official state filings, provided in a bound kit with the company name printed on the spine.

For customers who formed their company with us, the kit includes the company's Articles of Organization or Articles of Incorporation, the Statement of the Organizer, and the EIN confirmation, if applicable. Customers who did not form their company with us will receive the same organizational kit and materials, excluding the formation documents, as those records are not on file.
