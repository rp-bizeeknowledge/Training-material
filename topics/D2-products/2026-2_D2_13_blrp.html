Registration vs License vs Permit

Registration

Creates your legal business. Protects your personal assets.

Click to learn more

You exist. Your business is legally formed and personal assets are protected, but you're not yet authorized to sell or operate.

Business License

Allows you to start selling or offering services legally.

Click to learn more

You can start operating. With a license, you're authorized to sell products or offer services in your jurisdiction.

Business Permit

Permission to do specific things safely and legally.

Click to learn more

Here's how you must operate. Permits cover specific activities (food service, construction, zoning, and more).
Examples

Restaurant Owner

Phase 1: Legal Formation
  • Establishes LLC to create a recognized legal entity and secure liability protection.
Phase 2: Licensing Authorization
  • Secures a business license from the local government to begin operations.
Phase 3: Regulatory Compliance
  • Health Permit: Mandatory for preparing and serving food to the public.
  • Liquor Permit: Required to serve or sell alcoholic beverages.

Handyman Service

Phase 1: Legal Formation
  • Registers "Fix It Fast LLC" to operate under a formal business structure and shield personal assets.
Phase 2: Licensing Authorization
  • Applies for a business license with the city or county to offer general services legally.
Phase 3: Regulatory Compliance
  • Contractor's Permit: Required when performing structural, electrical, or plumbing work.
  • Zoning Permit: Ensures the business is allowed to operate from a residential or mixed-use location.
