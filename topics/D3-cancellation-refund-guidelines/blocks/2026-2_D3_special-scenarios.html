Subscription Services
Conditional

Virtual Address (VA)

VA subscriptions are cancelled from the Virtual Address Tab and refunded from Transaction History. A refund is only applicable for the unused portion of the billing period. If the customer has already used the VA for correspondence or mail forwarding in the current cycle, the refund may be pro-rated or denied depending on the policy in effect at the time. Confirm the billing date before processing.

Conditional

Registered Agent (RA)

RA subscriptions are cancelled from the Agent Tab. Refunds are processed from Transaction History and apply to the unused portion of the current billing period. Note that cancelling RA does not file a Change of Agent with the state — the customer must separately order that service if needed. Inform them accordingly.

Conditional

State Filings Bundle (SFB)

The SFB includes multiple services. Partial cancellations require supervisor approval. Full bundle cancellations can proceed if no individual service within it has been initiated. Cancel from the Subscriptions Tab and refund from Transaction History.

Conditional

Bizee Tax

Bizee Tax is a subscription service. Refund eligibility depends on whether the customer's tax return preparation has begun. If no work has started, cancel and refund. If work has started, escalate to the Tax Department before processing any refund.

Conditional

Domain & Email

Domain registrations are generally non-refundable once the domain has been registered with the registrar. Email service cancellations follow the same billing-period logic as VA. Cancel from the Subscriptions Tab and check Transaction History for any eligible pro-rated amount.

Conditional

Business Banking

Business Banking referrals are non-refundable as they are a referral service, not a direct product. If the customer was charged incorrectly, escalate to the billing team for review.

IRS-Filed Services
Non-Refundable

EIN

Once an EIN has been assigned by the IRS, the service is non-refundable. If the SS-4 has been faxed, escalate before processing any refund. If the EIN process has not yet started and the order is still in Review, a cancellation and refund may be possible.

Non-Refundable After Fax

Form 2553 (S-Corp Election)

Once the Form 2553 has been faxed to the IRS, the service is non-refundable. If faxing has not occurred, the order may be cancelled and refunded from the Order Status Dropdown.

Co-Sourced Services
Non-Refundable After Delivery

Trademark (Co-S)

Trademark orders are sent to an external law firm. Once the attorney has been assigned and initial contact has been made (typically within 48 hours), the service cannot be refunded. If the customer reaches out before attorney contact is made, escalate immediately for possible cancellation.

Non-Refundable After Report Delivery

BLRP (Co-S)

The Business License Research Package is fulfilled by Wolters Kluwer. Once the research report has been delivered to the customer's dashboard (approximately 3 business days), the service is non-refundable. If delivery has not occurred, cancel and refund from the Order Status Dropdown.
