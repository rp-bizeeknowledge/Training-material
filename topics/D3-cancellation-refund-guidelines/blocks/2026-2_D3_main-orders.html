Main Orders
Cancellation & Refund

Rules · Status-Based Eligibility · Step-by-Step Process
2.1 — Rules
✗

To resolve an Identity Confirmation Issue, escalate to your supervisor using the "Identity Confirmation Issue – Order cancellation request" macro. Do not mark the issue as resolved on your own.

⚠️

If the order has an ongoing Order Issue (name not available or an additional information request), click on "Resolve Issue" before you cancel and refund the order, or the system will not allow you to proceed.

⚠️

A 3 to 5-business-day refund timeframe begins after the refund is applied through the Transaction History Tab. Communicate this to the customer so they know when to expect the funds.

✗

Do not provide Cancellation or Refund confirmation until either process is complete.

2.2 — Steps
1

Go through the Comment Section and Order History Tab. You cannot move forward if there's any indication that the order has been submitted to the state or a comment stating the order should not be cancelled or refunded.

2

Check the Order Status and Order History Tab:

Pending Completion

The Order can be cancelled and refunded.

Review

The Order can be cancelled and refunded.

Processing

▷ If there's a "Start Automation" comment: Do not cancel or refund.
▷ If forwarded to the state: Do not cancel or refund.
▷ If not forwarded to the state: Cancel and refund.

Hold

▷ If forwarded to the state: Do not cancel or refund.
▷ If not forwarded to the state: Cancel and refund only if there are no comments stating the order should not be cancelled or refunded.

Forwarded / Shipped

Do not cancel or refund.

3

Refresh DB/Bizee Tech by pressing F5 on your keyboard, expand the Status dropdown from the panel on the left and click on the Cancel/Refund option.

4

Leave a clear comment about the actions taken, along with an internal note on your ticket.
