D3 — Section 3

Miscellaneous Orders
Cancellation & Refund

Product-Specific Rules · Special Scenarios · SFB Bundle
3.1 — Rules
⚠️

Always check the order history and DB comments before taking action. If the order has already been processed, submitted, or forwarded, it may not be eligible for cancellation.

✗

Do not cancel or refund any order that shows evidence of work having started or been submitted to the relevant authority.

✓

If the product order is in a pending or review state and no work has started, you may cancel and refund from the Order Status Dropdown.

3.2 — Product-Specific Rules
Annual Report
Can be cancelled and refunded if not yet submitted to the state. Check if the filing period has begun and if any state fees have already been paid.
Amendment
Can be cancelled and refunded if work has not started. If the order shows a "Start Automation" comment or has been forwarded to the state, do not cancel or refund.
Change of Agent
Can be cancelled and refunded if not yet filed with the state.
Certificate of Good Standing
Can be cancelled and refunded if the certificate has not yet been retrieved from the state.
Dissolution
Can be cancelled and refunded if not yet filed. If filed, cancellation is not possible as the dissolution cannot be undone.
Reinstatement
Can be cancelled and refunded if the filing has not yet been submitted to the state.
Foreign Qualification
Can be cancelled and refunded if the COGS has not been retrieved and the application has not been submitted.
EIN
Cannot be refunded once the EIN has been assigned by the IRS. If the SS-4 has been submitted, escalate before processing any refund.
DBA
Can be cancelled and refunded if not yet filed at the state or county level.
Form 2553
Cannot be refunded once the form has been faxed to the IRS.
Business Contract Templates
Non-refundable once the customer has accessed the templates through their dashboard.
BLRP
Non-refundable once the research report has been delivered to the customer's dashboard.
Business Formation Kit
Non-refundable once the kit has been shipped. If not yet shipped, cancel and refund.
3.3 — SFB Bundle Special Scenario

If a customer has purchased the State Filings Bundle (SFB) and wants to cancel individual components, note the following:

⚠️

The SFB is a bundled product. Partial cancellations of individual services within the bundle require supervisor approval. Do not process partial refunds without confirming eligibility for each component within the bundle.

If the customer wants to cancel the entire bundle and none of the services have been initiated, you may proceed with a full cancellation and refund from the Order Status Dropdown.
