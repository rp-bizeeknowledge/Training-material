The Dashboard · Topic 03

Single-View Layout

When it shows · Key elements · Default login tabs · Section breakdown
What It Is

The Single-View layout will show after clicking "View Company" from the Multi-View list, or by default for accounts linked to only one company/only one PVA. When accessing, users will see essential company details, pending actions, and navigation options.

Overview

The Single-View is designed to show company-specific information, and allow customers to order services and manage access and contact information about the selected business. Below are the key elements from this layout.

1

Company Name Display

At the top of the page, the company name will be prominently displayed. This ensures users always know which company's details they are viewing.

2

Action Items Banner

If any action items require attention, a banner will appear at the very top of the page. This serves as a prompt for the client to take necessary actions.

3

Contact Information (Top Left Corner)

On the upper left-hand side, users will see:

  • Contact Person: The primary contact for the company.
  • Contact Email: The email address associated with the contact person.
  • Order Number: A reference number for tracking the company's registration or service package.
4

Quick Action Buttons (Top Right Corner)

  • Start a New Company: Allows the user to place an order for a new company package.
  • Logout: Enables users to safely log out of the dashboard.
5

Expanded Left-Hand Side Menu

When entering the Single View, the left-side menu will expand, displaying different navigation options.

  • Dashboard: A standalone tab that returns users to the main dashboard overview.
  • Settings: Another standalone tab that provides access to account settings and configurations.

Below these two tabs, there are four sections that contain additional tabs:

  • Actions: Displays tasks that require user attention.
  • Company Documents: Provides access to relevant documents associated with the company.
  • Company Details: Displays key company information, such as registration details.
  • Services: Lists available services or packages related to company management.
Default Single-View Login

Right after the customer signs in, the system will automatically pre-select one of the tabs from the Actions Section, depending on the specific account status:

Pending Issue Tab

Shows Action Items that require immediate attention and helps customers address issues that prevent products and services from being rendered or activated.

My Tasks Tab

Shows included and unclaimed services and benefits for customers to take full advantage of included features.

Recommendations Tab

Shows additional services and products available to the customer, these are usually not required right away, but allow customers to have quick access to common requests.

Compliance Tab

Serves as a reminder platform, showing upcoming compulsory filing deadlines to help customers stay compliant.

Section Breakdown
Company Documents Section

Clients can access official company documents and manage additional document-related services.

Company Documents

Completed Documents Tab

Shows all finalized documents, such as:

  • Filed Articles
  • EIN confirmation
  • Statement of the Organizer/Incorporator
  • IRS Form 2553
  • Meeting Minutes
  • Corporate Bylaws
  • Operating Agreement
  • Banking Resolution
Virtual Mailbox Tab

Available mail from the Registered Agent and Virtual Address will be found here. Clients can:

  • View scanned mail
  • Subscribe to the Virtual Address (if not already subscribed)
  • Purchase an additional Virtual Address
Business Contract Templates Tab

Clients can:

  • Download and modify the business contract templates if they have purchased the bundle
  • Purchase the business contract templates bundle if they haven't already
  • Request a custom-drafted contract with an attorney (for an additional fee): the attorney contacts the client to discuss the details before drafting the document. Only available to customers who have already purchased the business contract templates.
Company Details Section

Displays essential company information and allows some updates.

Company Details

Company Info Tab

Displays: Company name, Owners, Addresses, EIN, Contact details. Note: Only the contact information is editable from this tab.

Registered Agent Tab

Shows current Registered Agent details (either Bizee, Entity, or Individual). Clients can: Subscribe to Registered Agent service, or Change (appoint) a different Registered Agent.

Order History / Receipts Tab

Displays all purchases and subscriptions related to the specific company.

Compliance Tab

Duplicate of the Compliance tab found in Actions (if active). Shows deadlines for filings and notifications to maintain legal compliance.
