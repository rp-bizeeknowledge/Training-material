Multi-View Layout

Navigating between multiple companies from a single dashboard
What It Is

The Multi-View layout is a dashboard view that allows customers with multiple companies or PVAs under the same account to select which company they want to access and manage during their session.

Overview

This layout displays general account information and provides quick access to received mail and contact management tools.

For customers with multiple companies registered through us, this screen serves as a central hub where they can choose which entity they would like to access. Because company-specific information is not displayed until a selection is made, customers often believe records, documents, or services are missing from their account.

Recognizing the Multi-View layout will help you quickly identify this situation and guide customers to the correct layout, where all information associated with the selected company will be available.

1

Action Items

This section highlights tasks that require the customer's attention, such as:

  • Upcoming annual reports that need to be filed
  • Pending signatures
  • Expired services that require renewal
2

My Companies

Displays a list of all the companies the customer has registered with us. Customers can select a company from this list to access its Single-view, where they can manage details and request additional services.

3

Order Services for Companies Not Registered with Bizee

Displays stand-alone services that customers can purchase for companies not registered with us.

Section Breakdown

Customers will find the following options on the left-hand side menu while on Multi-view:

1

Dashboard

Returns the customer to the Multi-view screen.

2

Settings

Allows customers to update and manage their contact information and payment methods.

3

Virtual Mailbox

Provides access to scanned mail if the customer is subscribed to a Registered Agent or Virtual Address service.

4

All Orders/Receipts

Displays a record of all purchases made through our system, including receipts.

Switching to Single-View

Customers can select the company they want to work on from the Dashboard tab by clicking the "View Company" button next to the corresponding company.
