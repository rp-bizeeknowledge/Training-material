D2 — Section 14 (Co-Sourced)

Business
Formation Kit

FastKits · FedEx Delivery · Bound Document Set · $99 Add-On
What It Is

A document set designed to organize and store essential business records, including official state filings, provided in a bound kit with the company name printed on the spine.

For customers who formed their company with us, the kit includes the company's Articles of Organization or Articles of Incorporation, the Statement of the Organizer, and the EIN confirmation, if applicable. Customers who did not form their company with us will receive the same organizational kit and materials, excluding the formation documents, as those records are not on file.

$99 — not to be confused with the $25 mailed copies (those are not in a kit)
14.1 — Notes
Kits are shipped via FedEx Ground the next business day after order completion, arriving within 3–4 business days in the continental U.S.
The kit is an outsourced service provided by FastKits.
⚙️ Internal Only — Do Not Share with Customer

This kit was previously included with the Premium plan, formerly known as Platinum. It's now available as a $99 add-on exclusively for existing customers and can only be purchased through the dashboard — not directly from the website. Do not disclose the FastKits provider name to customers.
