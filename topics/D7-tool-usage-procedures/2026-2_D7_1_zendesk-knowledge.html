Zendesk Knowledge Base (KB)

Introduction to our source of knowledge

Before starting this section, complete the required Zendesk Training Courses provided by your instructor.

What It Is

The Knowledge Base (KB) is a Zendesk-powered tool that serves as Bizee's centralized source of documented knowledge, designed to help agents quickly find relevant, accurate, and up-to-date information while assisting customers. It contains structured articles that explain processes, products, policies, and common customer scenarios, serving as the primary reference point for day-to-day work within the Support Team.

How to Access

KB is available whenever you are signed in to Zendesk. Agents can consult it while assisting a customer using the methods below.

Direct Link

Visiting incfile.zendesk.com/hc/en-us will take you to KB's main page. Agents may bookmark this page to keep it readily available for quicker reference during daily work.

KB Article Search

This path also leads to KB's main page, where you can run a global search for article titles you are already familiar with or enter relevant keywords to receive suggested articles.

AI-Suggested Articles Integration

You can access auto-generated suggestions based on the ticket's content. This tool might not be fully accurate as it's based on AI detection of content; refer to the global search if unsure.

AI Quick Answers

KB supports content summaries and article suggestions when searches include more than two keywords. For better accuracy, use clear and concise search terms that best describe the issue you are trying to resolve.

AI Quick Answers · Example

Agents should always review the suggested content carefully and confirm that it applies to the specific customer scenario. Even when search results appear relevant, it is important to verify details within the full article to ensure accuracy and proper context before providing guidance to the customer. These are only suggestions.

Navigating an Article

Most KB Articles include an introduction and an index, followed by the full content. When resolving a query, take a moment to review the "IN THIS ARTICLE" section first and select the relevant hyperlink to jump directly to the appropriate section.

AI Quick Answers · Example

Using Ctrl + F is strongly recommended, as it allows you to quickly scan the entire article and locate relevant information more efficiently.
