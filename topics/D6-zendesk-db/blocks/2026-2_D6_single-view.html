When Single-View Appears

The Single-View layout will show after clicking "View Company" from the Multi-View list, or by default for accounts linked to only one company or only one PVA. When accessing, users will see essential company details, pending actions, and navigation options.

3.1 — Key Elements
1

Company Name Display

At the top of the page, the company name will be prominently displayed. This ensures users always know which company's details they are viewing.

2

Action Items Banner

If any action items require attention, a banner will appear at the very top of the page as a prompt for the client to take necessary actions.

3

Contact Information (Top Left)

Contact Person, Contact Email, and Order Number are displayed on the upper left-hand side.

4

Quick Action Buttons (Top Right)

Start a New Company (place an order for a new company package) and Logout buttons.

5

Expanded Left-Hand Side Menu

When entering the Single View, the left-side menu will expand, displaying: Dashboard (returns to main overview), Settings (account settings and configurations), and four sections below: Actions, Company Documents, Company Details, and Services.

3.2 — Default Single-View Login Tabs

Right after the customer signs in, the system will automatically pre-select one of the tabs from the Actions Section, depending on the specific account status:

Pending Issue Tab

Shows Action Items that require immediate attention and helps customers address issues that prevent products and services from being rendered or activated.

My Tasks Tab

Shows included and unclaimed services and benefits for customers to take full advantage of included features.

Recommendations Tab

Shows additional services and products available to the customer. These are usually not required right away but allow customers to have quick access to common requests.

Compliance Tab

Serves as a reminder platform, showing upcoming compulsory filing deadlines to help customers stay compliant.

3.3 — Section Breakdown

Company Documents Section

Completed Documents Tab

Shows all finalized documents, such as:

  • Filed Articles
  • EIN confirmation
  • Statement of the Organizer/Incorporator
  • IRS Form 2553
  • Meeting Minutes, Corporate Bylaws, Operating Agreement, Banking Resolution
Virtual Mailbox Tab
  • View scanned mail from RA and VA
  • Subscribe to Virtual Address (if not already)
  • Purchase an additional Virtual Address
Business Contract Templates Tab
  • Download and modify templates if purchased
  • Purchase the bundle if not yet purchased
  • Request a custom-drafted contract with an attorney (additional fee — attorney contacts client first; only available to customers who already purchased the templates)

Company Details Section

Company Info Tab

Displays company name, owners, addresses, EIN, and contact details. Only the contact information is editable from this tab.

Registered Agent Tab

Shows current Registered Agent details (Bizee, Entity, or Individual). Clients can subscribe to RA service or change (appoint) a different Registered Agent.

Order History / Receipts Tab

Displays all purchases and subscriptions related to the specific company.

Compliance Tab

Duplicate of the Compliance tab found in Actions (if active). Shows deadlines for filings and notifications to maintain legal compliance.
