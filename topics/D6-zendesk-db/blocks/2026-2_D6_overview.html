What It Is

The Bizee Dashboard is a centralized online workspace where customers can access and manage their business documents, orders, and services all in one place. It highlights recommended products and services based on the business's needs, helping customers stay organized and compliant with state regulations.

1.1 — Accessing the Dashboard

To access the Dashboard, customers log in to Bizee.com using their email address and a one-time access code they can choose to receive via email or phone.

🔐

Passwords are not used. Authentication is always via a one-time code sent to email or phone.

1.2 — Main Sections

The following sections are always visible when navigating the Dashboard, regardless of the view type displayed or selected:

Quick Action Buttons

Let customers log out, order a new company formation, or contact the support team through phone from anywhere in the dashboard.

Left Hand-Side Menu

Divided into tabs contained into sections. This menu changes depending on the view type, showing available options for a specific company (in single-view mode) or overall account management (in multi-view mode). Customers will only see company-specific information and tabs after entering the single-view.

Main Content Section

Displays the selected tab's information. It is constantly changing according to the user's selection, and serves as the information output.
