4.1 — Bizee PRO (Commercial Clients)

A Commercial Client is an individual or third party with multiple companies linked to their Bizee account. Most commonly, these clients resell our services and assist others with business formation using our platform and their own information for filing purposes (accountants, lawyers, CPAs, and business formation companies). Commercial clients should always receive top-tier support, as they are recurring customers we aim to retain.

Our system automatically labels a client as commercial in the following scenarios:

Whenever an account places 10 main orders or more
If the customer has characteristics that determine they are an agent using our services to conduct their business (e.g., an email ending in @createyourllc.com)

Bizee PRO is a dedicated program for commercial clients that allows them to use our filing services on behalf of their own clients and manage everything under one multi-company dashboard. They have flexibility to keep full access, provide individual logins, or transfer accounts to their clients. They can also set unique payment methods per company if needed.

Special Pricing

$99 for the Standard Package (instead of $199) + state fees

Dedicated Support

A dedicated phone and email account coordinator for ongoing support

Expedited Filing

Expedited filing fee automatically included (cannot be removed)

⚠️

Life Time Value (LTV): There is a section under the client tab in DB called LTV which stands for Life Time Value. This section shows the profit we have made with that specific client. If the LTV is $300 or above, we must provide superlative customer service treatment. These are the clients we want to be extra pleasing to. If they want to do something we usually don't do, consult it with your supervisor.

4.2 — Affiliate Program

Affiliates earn commissions by recommending our brand to their customers. This program is managed by a third party called AWIN, not by our internal support. If a customer asks about the affiliate program:

Inform them that the affiliate program is managed externally by a third party.
Provide the link: bizee.com/affiliates
If they need further assistance, they can contact the affiliate team directly at affiliates@incfile.com
Affiliates vs. Collabs

If the inquiry is about inserting a link on our website, writing a blog post, or other collaboration requests that do not involve earning through the affiliate program, this is not an affiliate request. Such cases should be treated as Collaboration requests.

4.3 — Collaboration & Advertisement Requests

Examples: Bloggers or influencers interested in collaborating, guest post or partnership inquiries, proposals to promote Bizee in exchange for link placement or visibility.

What to do:

Assign to Cassie in Zendesk by updating the assignee and submitting with a Pending status — as long as it's not an affiliate request and not from a no-reply email.
If it's related to affiliates, please use the affiliate program macro.
If it's spam, add an internal note and solve the ticket.
