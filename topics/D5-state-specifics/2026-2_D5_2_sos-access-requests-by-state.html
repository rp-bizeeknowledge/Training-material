SOS Access Requests by State

Giving customer access to their company's account online
Overview

We support customer access requests for certain Secretary of State (SOS) online business accounts. In these states, customers can request credentials to manage a company we formed directly through the SOS website. For states not listed below, customers should contact their Secretary of State office directly for access instructions.

General Procedure

When a customer contacts us asking to access their company account on the SOS site, follow these steps:

  1. Direct the customer to complete the state-specific prerequisite before proceeding. Each state's requirement is listed in the sections below.
  2. Once they have done so, ask them to provide:
    • Full name used for the SOS request or account
    • Email address used for the SOS request or account
  3. Once you receive that information, send the following message:

    Thank you for sharing this information. I'll escalate this request to the correct department to grant access. As soon as I have an update regarding this matter, I'll be sure to notify you.

  4. Escalate to your supervisor, including the following in the internal note:
    • Order number and company name
    • Requester's name
    • Email used
    • Brief description of the request
  5. Once access is confirmed, notify the customer that they now have access to their company information on the SOS website.
South Dakota

Direct the customer to request access from the South Dakota Secretary of State's website before proceeding, then follow the General Procedure above.

North Dakota

Direct the customer to create an account on the North Dakota Secretary of State's website using their name and email address. Once the account is created, they can log in and click the request access option. Then follow the General Procedure above.

Arizona

Direct the customer to create an account on the Arizona Secretary of State's website using their name and email address. Then follow the General Procedure above.

Utah

Direct the customer to create a Utah-ID account within the SOS website before proceeding. Then follow the General Procedure above.

California

Direct the customer to create an account on the California Secretary of State's website using their name and email address. Once the account is created, they can log in and click the request access option. Then follow the General Procedure above.

If Bizee Is Requesting Access on the Customer's Behalf
  1. Explain that this is part of a new CA SOS system request.
  2. The customer must call the state using the number provided in the issue email and authorize access for Bizee.
  3. The state will provide a PIN or other confirmation.
  4. The customer must send this PIN back to us by replying to the issue email or to support@bizee.com.
  5. Escalate this information to the Order Issues department. No further action is needed after escalation.
